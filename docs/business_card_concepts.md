# Business Card Design Concepts

**Project:** Vayne Consultations & Creations
**Date:** 2026-01-25

Three distinct design directions tailored to the "Modern Polymath" brand identity, ranging from ultra-minimalist to high-tech and classic luxury.

---

## Concept 1: "The Void" (Minimalist & Mysterious)

*Focus on exclusivity and curiosity.*

* **Front:**
  * **Background:** Deepest Matte Black possible (Onyx).
  * **Element:** A single, large "V" logo centered.
  * **Finish:** Spot UV (High Gloss) Red on the logo against the Matte Black background. No text.
* **Back:**
  * **Background:** Matte Black.
  * **Typography:** White, clean sans-serif (Inter or Helvetica), small point size (8pt), bottom-left aligned.
  * **Content:**
        > **VAYNE**
        > Proprietor
        > vayne.consulting
* **Material:** 32pt Silk Matte Stock. Rigid and velvety to the touch.

---

## Concept 2: "The Signal" (Tech & Polymath)

*Focus on the digital, gaming, and security verticals.*

* **Front:**
  * **Background:** Dark Charcoal (#1a1a1a).
  * **Element:** A thin, vertical neon-red line running from top to bottom (off-center right).
  * **Typography:** "Vayne Consultations" in a modern, monospaced font (JetBrains Mono or similar) positioned vertically along the red line.
  * **Effect:** Subtle header glitch effect on the "V".
* **Back:**
  * **Background:** Black with a very faint circuit-board pattern in dark grey (5% opacity).
  * **Element:** A Custom Red QR Code in the center.
  * **Text:** "INITIATE_CONTACT" below the QR code in micro-text.
* **Material:** PVC (Plastic) Card or Metal Hybrid (Black Metal).

---

## Concept 3: "The Manuscript" (Classic & Authority)

*Focus on "The Red Book," Writing, and High-End Consulting.*

* **Front:**
  * **Background:** Heavily Textured Black Cardstock (Linen or Cotton finish).
  * **Typography:** "Vayne" in an elegant Serif font (Crimson Pro or EB Garamond - matching the book).
  * **Finish:** Deep Red Metallic Foil Embossing for the text.
* **Back:**
  * **Background:** Smooth Black.
  * **Content:** Centered, Gold Foil text in small caps:
        > MUSIC • CULINARY • GAMES • SECURITY
        >
        > *vayne.consulting*
* **Material:** 40pt Cotton Paper with **Red Edge Painting** (the sides of the card are red).
