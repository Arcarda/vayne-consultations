# Pricing Proposal & Service Structure

**Client:** Vayne Consultations & Creations
**Owner & Proprietor:** Vayne
**Date:** January 22, 2026
**Currency:** CAD $

## Executive Summary

This proposal outlines the pricing structure for Vayne Consultations' digital presence and consultation services. Estimates are based on current Canadian market standards for high-end web development and specialized industry consulting.

## 1. Web Development & Deployment (One-Time Costs)

### **Package A: The "Chic" Launch (Current Scope)**

*A high-impact, single-page application focused on brand identity and core verticals (Music, Food, Games).*

* **Design & UI/UX**: Custom "Red/Black" luxury aesthetic, responsive mobile design, high-fidelity mockups.
* **Development**: HTML5/CSS3 (Advanced Animations), Vanilla JS interactivity, Optimized performance.
* **Content Strategy**: Integration of 3 core sections.
* **Estimated Cost**: **$3,500 - $5,000 CAD**
* **Timeline**: 2-3 Weeks

### **Package B: The "Advanced" Ecosystem (Future Expansion)**

*Multi-page architecture, CMS integration, booking systems, and dynamic content loading.*

* **Features**: User accounts, persistent booking database, admin dashboard, SEO optimization suite.
* **Estimated Cost**: **$8,000 - $12,000+ CAD**

### **Hourly Development Rate**

For maintenance, updates, or features outside the fixed scope:

* **Standard Rate**: **$120/hr** (Senior Developer)

## 2. Consultation Services Pricing Models

### **Phase 1: Discovery & Strategy (Complimentary)**

* **Initial Consultation**: **$0.00 (Free)**
  * *Scope*: Preliminary assessment of business goals, feasibility check, and initial expansion concepts.
  * *Note*: Subject to review and approval of application.
  * *Duration*: Up to 2 sessions.

### **Phase 2: Execution & Expansion (Contractual)**

*All subsequent work is billed based on a confirmed scope of work and signed contractual agreement.*

**Model A: Strategic Expansion & Advisory**

* **Retainer Fee**: Determined by scope (starting at $2,500/mo).
* **Hourly Overage**: **$150 - $250/hr** (depending on vertical).

**Model B: Growth Partnership (Project-Based)**

* **Full Launch & Expansion Suite**: Quoted per project.
  * *Typical Range*: **$5,000 - $15,000+**
  * *Includes*: Complete go-to-market strategy, vendor partner setup, promotional campaign management, and operational scaling.

**Model C: Information Security (SMB Specialization)**

* **Vulnerability Assessment**: **$1,500 - $3,000** (Per Instance)
  * *Includes*: External/Internal scanning, risk report, and remediation roadmap.
* **Full Security Audit & Policy Framework**: **$4,500+**
  * *Includes*: Comprehensive penetration testing, staff security training, and compliance policy creation (PII/GDPR).

## 3. Deployment & Hosting (Recurring)

* **Domain Name**: ~$20 - ~$50/year
* **High-Performance Hosting (Vercel/Netlify/DigitalOcean)**: ~$20 - ~$40/month
* **Maintenance Retainer (Optional)**: **$300/month** (Includes updates, backups, and minor content tweaks).
